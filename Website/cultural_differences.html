    Referencing

    Cultural Differences

    You must demonstrate your reading by citing ideas from the original source. Citations are an academic method of acknowledging an author's work. However, plagiarism is the opposite in which the user does not credit their inspiration to the original author. Serious consequences are involved in plagiarism. However, plagiarism can easily be avoided with a few small steps.
